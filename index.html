About Prudy

Prudy is a dedicated and passionate dietician committed to helping individuals improve their health through practical, personalised nutrition. She believes that healthy eating should be sustainable, enjoyable, and tailored to each person’s lifestyle — not restrictive or overwhelming.

With a client-centred approach, Prudy works closely with individuals to understand their unique needs, health goals, and challenges. Her focus is on creating realistic nutrition strategies that support long-term wellness, whether the goal is weight management, improved energy, or better overall health.

Prudy is passionate about empowering her clients with knowledge and guidance, helping them build a positive relationship with food and confidence in their choices.
