Simple Nutrition Tips

  • Don’t skip meals: Maintain energy and prevent overeating.
  • Stay hydrated: Water supports digestion and energy.
  • Balance your plate: Include vegetables, protein & whole grains.
  • Listen to your body: Eat mindfully and stop when satisfied.
  • Small changes matter: Consistency leads to long-term results.
